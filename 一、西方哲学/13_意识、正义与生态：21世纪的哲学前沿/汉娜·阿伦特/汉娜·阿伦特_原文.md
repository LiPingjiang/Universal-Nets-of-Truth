# 汉娜·阿伦特（政治与恶）_原文

## 一、极权主义的本质

> Totalitarianism strives not toward despotic rule at all, but toward a system in which men are superfluous.
> ——《极权主义的起源》

**甲译**：极权主义根本不是朝着专制统治迈进，而是朝着一个人成为多余的体系迈进。

**乙译**：Totalitarianism（极权主义）strives（力求）not toward despotic rule（专制统治）at all，but toward a system（体系）in which men are superfluous（多余的）——极权主义力求的不是专制统治，而是使人成为多余的体系。

---

> The real horror of the concentration and extermination camps lies in the fact that the inmates, even if they happen to keep alive, are more effectively cut off from the world of the living than if they had died.
> ——《极权主义的起源》

**甲译**：集中营和灭绝营的真正恐怖在于，囚犯们即使碰巧活了下来，也比死去更有效地被切断了与活人世界的联系。

**乙译**：The real horror（真正恐怖）of the concentration and extermination camps（集中营和灭绝营）lies in the fact（在于）that the inmates（囚犯），even if they happen to keep alive（即使碰巧存活），are more effectively cut off（更有效切断）from the world of the living（活人世界）than if they had died（比死去更甚）。

## 二、恶之平庸

> The trouble with Eichmann was precisely that so many were like him, and that the many were neither perverted nor sadistic, that they were, and still are, terribly and terrifyingly normal.
> ——《艾希曼在耶路撒冷》

**甲译**：艾希曼的问题恰恰在于，有太多人像他一样，而这些人既非变态，亦非虐待狂，他们过去是、现在仍然是可怕的、令人恐惧的正常人。

**乙译**：The trouble with Eichmann（艾希曼的问题）was precisely（恰恰）that so many were like him（有太多人像他），and that the many were neither perverted nor sadistic（既非变态亦非虐待狂），that they were, and still are（过去是、现在仍然是），terribly and terrifyingly normal（可怕的、令人恐惧的正常）。

---

> It was as though in those last minutes he was summing up the lesson that this long course in human wickedness had taught us—the lesson of the fearsome, word-and-thought-defying banality of evil.
> ——《艾希曼在耶路撒冷》

**甲译**：仿佛在最后几分钟，他在总结这漫长的人类邪恶课程教给我们的教训——那令人恐惧、难以用言语和思想把握的恶之平庸的教训。

**乙译**：It was as though（仿佛）in those last minutes（在最后几分钟）he was summing up（总结）the lesson（教训）that this long course in human wickedness（漫长的人类邪恶课程）had taught us（教给我们）——the lesson（教训）of the fearsome, word-and-thought-defying（令人恐惧、难以用言语和思想把握）banality of evil（恶之平庸）。

## 三、劳动、工作、行动

> Labor is the activity which corresponds to the biological process of the human body... Work is the activity which corresponds to the unnaturalness of human existence... Action, the only activity that goes on directly between men without the intermediary of things or matter, corresponds to the human condition of plurality.
> ——《人的境况》

**甲译**：劳动是对应于人体生物过程的活动……工作是对应于人类存在非自然性的活动……行动是唯一直接在人与人之间进行、无需事物或物质中介的活动，它对应于人的复数性处境。

**乙译**：Labor（劳动）is the activity（活动）which corresponds to（对应于）the biological process（生物过程）of the human body（人体）……Work（工作）is the activity which corresponds to the unnaturalness（非自然性）of human existence（人类存在）……Action（行动）, the only activity that goes on directly between men（直接在人与人之间进行）without the intermediary of things or matter（无需事物或物质中介），corresponds to（对应于）the human condition of plurality（人的复数性处境）。

---

## 四、诞生性

> The new beginning inherent in birth can make itself felt in the world only because the newcomer possesses the capacity of beginning something anew, that is, of acting.
> ——《人的境况》

**甲译**：诞生所固有的新的开始之所以能在世界中显现，仅仅因为 newcomer（新来者）拥有开始某种新事物的能力，即，行动的能力。

**乙译**：The new beginning（新的开始）inherent in birth（诞生所固有的）can make itself felt（显现）in the world（在世界中）only because（仅仅因为）the newcomer（新来者）possesses the capacity（拥有能力）of beginning something anew（开始新事物），that is（即），of acting（行动）。

---

## 五、判断的能力

> The ability to judge, to tell right from wrong, beautiful from ugly, is the most specifically human capacity.
> ——《思想与道德关切》

**甲译**：判断的能力——分辨对错、美丑——是最具人类特异性的能力。

**乙译**：The ability to judge（判断的能力），to tell right from wrong（分辨对错），beautiful from ugly（美丑），is the most specifically human capacity（最具人类特异性的能力）。
