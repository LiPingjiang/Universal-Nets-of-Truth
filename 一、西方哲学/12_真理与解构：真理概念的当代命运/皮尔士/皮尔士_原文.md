# 皮尔士：如何使我们的观念清晰

## 一、实用主义格言（The Pragmatic Maxim）

> **甲译**：一个概念的意义，就是它在实践中会产生什么效果。如果两个概念在任何可能的实践中都产生相同的效果，那它们就是同一个概念。
> 乙译：实用主义格言（Pragmatic Maxim）是皮尔士实用主义的核心原则：「考虑一下，我们所设想的概念的对象，会产生什么样的实际效果——这些效果，就是我们对这个对象的概念的全部内容。」（Consider what effects, that might conceivably have practical bearings, we conceive the object of our conception to have. Then, our conception of these effects is the whole of our conception of the object.）

**原文**：

> "Consider what effects, that might conceivably have practical bearings, we conceive the object of our conception to have. Then, our conception of these effects is the whole of our conception of the object."[^peirce-1]

> "It appears, then, that the rule for attaining the third grade of clearness of apprehension is as follows: Consider what effects, that might conceivably have practical bearings, we conceive the object of our conception to have. Then, our conception of these effects is the whole of our conception of the object."[^peirce-2]

## 二、真理作为探究共同体的最终意见（Truth as the Final Opinion）

> **甲译**：真理不是某个人在某个时刻的信念，而是所有认真探究的人，在无限长的探究过程结束时，最终会一致同意的那个信念。
> 乙译：皮尔士将真理定义为「探究共同体在无限探究过程中最终会一致同意的意见」（the opinion which is fated to be ultimately agreed to by all who investigate）。真理是探究的极限，而非探究的起点。

**原文**：

> "The opinion which is fated to be ultimately agreed to by all who investigate, is what we mean by the truth, and the object represented in this opinion is the real."[^peirce-3]

> "Truth is that concordance of an abstract statement with the ideal limit towards which endless investigation would tend to bring scientific belief."[^peirce-4]

> "Different minds may set out with the most antagonistic views, but the progress of investigation carries them by a force outside of themselves to one and the same conclusion. This activity of thought by which we are carried, not where we wish, but to a fore-ordained goal, is like the operation of destiny."[^peirce-5]

## 三、信念、怀疑与探究（Belief, Doubt, and Inquiry）

> **甲译**：探究不是从零开始的——它从怀疑开始。当你的信念受到刺激、产生怀疑时，你才开始探究。探究的目的是消除怀疑、恢复信念。
> 乙译：皮尔士将探究（inquiry）定义为从怀疑（doubt）到信念（belief）的过程。信念是一种行动的习惯（habit of action），怀疑是信念的中断。探究的目的不是追求抽象的真理，而是消除怀疑、建立稳定的信念。

**原文**：

> "The irritation of doubt causes a struggle to attain a state of belief. I shall term this struggle *Inquiry*, though it must be admitted that this is sometimes not a very apt designation."[^peirce-6]

> "The feeling of believing is a more or less sure indication of there being established in our nature some habit which will determine our actions. Doubt never has such an effect."[^peirce-7]

> "The sole object of inquiry is the settlement of opinion. We may fancy that this is not enough for us, and that we seek, not merely an opinion, but a true opinion. But put this fancy to the test, and it proves groundless; for as soon as a firm belief is reached we are entirely satisfied, whether the belief be true or false."[^peirce-8]

## 四、固定信念的四种方法（Four Methods of Fixing Belief）

> **甲译**：人们固定信念有四种方法：坚持法（不管别人怎么说，我就是这么信）、权威法（权威说什么就信什么）、先验法（用理性推导出来的）、科学法（通过实验和观察来检验）。只有科学法才能真正固定信念，因为它依赖于独立于我们意志的外部实在。
> 乙译：皮尔士在《如何使我们的观念清晰》的姊妹篇《信念的固定》（The Fixation of Belief, 1877）中，区分了固定信念的四种方法：坚持法（tenacity）、权威法（authority）、先验法（a priori method）、科学法（scientific method）。只有科学法通过诉诸外部实在来固定信念，才能最终导向真理。

**原文**：

> "The method of science is distinguished from others by the fact that it is the only one which presents any distinction of a right and a wrong way. If I adopt the method of tenacity, and shut myself out from all influences, whatever I think necessary to doing this is necessary according to that method. So with the method of authority: the state may try to put down heresy by means which, from a scientific point of view, seem very ill-calculated to accomplish its purposes; but the only test on that method is what the state thinks; so that it cannot pursue the method wrongly."[^peirce-9]

> "To satisfy our doubts, therefore, it is necessary that a method should be found by which our beliefs may be determined by nothing human, but by some external permanency—by something upon which our thinking has no effect."[^peirce-10]

## 五、符号学与真理（Semiotics and Truth）

> **甲译**：真理不是心灵与世界的直接对应，而是通过符号（sign）的无限解释过程来实现的。每一个符号都指向另一个符号，真理是这个无限符号链的极限。
> 乙译：皮尔士的符号学（semiotics）将真理理解为符号过程（semiosis）的极限。符号（sign）、对象（object）和解释项（interpretant）构成三元关系。真理不是符号与对象的直接对应，而是在无限的解释过程中，解释项趋向稳定的极限状态。

**原文**：

> "A sign, or representamen, is something which stands to somebody for something in some respect or capacity. It addresses somebody, that is, creates in the mind of that person an equivalent sign, or perhaps a more developed sign. That sign which it creates I call the interpretant of the first sign."[^peirce-11]

> "The meaning of a representation can be nothing but a representation. In fact, it is nothing but the representation itself conceived as stripped of irrelevant clothing. But this clothing never can be completely stripped off; it is only changed for something more diaphanous. So there is an infinite regression here."[^peirce-12]
